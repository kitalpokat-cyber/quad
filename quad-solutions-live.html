What we're looking for

Four pillars of a great agent

You don't need to tick every box — but the more of these you bring, the better. Fluent English is a must.

Communication

Fluent English — speak and understand clearly and confidently.
Active listening — hear what customers really mean.

Composure

Calm under pressure — steady through busy days and tough calls.
Positive attitude — friendly, reliable, motivated to grow.

Problem solving

Quick thinking — find the right answer for each customer.
Computer literacy — comfortable across apps and systems.
